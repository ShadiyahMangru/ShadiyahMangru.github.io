Conversion Examples

1.) Convert the Binary number 1101101 to its Decimal equivalent.
1101101
= (1)*26 + (1)*25 + (0)*24 + (1)*23 + (1)*22 + (0)*21 + (1)*20
= 64 + 32 + 0 + 8 + 4 + 0 + 1
= 109

2.) Convert the Decimal number 100 to its Binary equivalent.
100 ÷ 2 = 50 R0
50 ÷ 2 = 25 R0
25 ÷ 2 = 12 R1
12 ÷ 2 = 6 R0
6 ÷ 2 = 3 R0
3 ÷ 2 = 1 R1
1 ÷ 2 = 0 R1
Therefore 100 (decimal) = 1100100 (binary)
