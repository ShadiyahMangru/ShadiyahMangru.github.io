War Rules

War is a card game between two players. Each player flips one card per 'battle' and the player with the high card wins. Here, King is a high card (=13) and Ace is a low card (=1).

During this mission:
  1. Press 'Play War' to start each battle.
  2. Identify the battle winner as either 'P1', 'P2', or 'Tie', by pressing the corresponding button. The objective is to maximize your Quantitative Comparisons score by correctly identifying the winner of each battle.
  3. Repeat steps 1 and 2 until no cards remain to be flipped. Good luck!
