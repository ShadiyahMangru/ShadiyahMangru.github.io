Let's Play War!
In this game, a standard 52-card deck is split between you and the dealer. Press 'War!' to start a round; this flips a card for each player. The player with the high card wins that round. Repeat this process until no cards remain and a winner is determined. In this game, King is a high card (=13) and Ace is a low card (=1). Good luck!
